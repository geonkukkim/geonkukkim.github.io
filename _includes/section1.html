About Me

Physical AI Engineer at COSMAX, focused on contact-rich cosmetics manufacturing robots, humanoid systems, and VLA-based action planning.

"Optimism always finds a way"

— Dennis Hong

I am a Physical AI R&D Engineer at COSMAX in Pangyo, where I develop contact-rich robotics for cosmetics manufacturing, including humanoid and dual-arm motion control, contact-based robot-hand control, robot mechanism design and system integration, AI-agent memory/planning HCI/HRI, and VLA-based robot action planning. Previously, I was a Physical AI Engineer at WIRobotics, where I developed VLA-based systems for contact-rich and long-horizon manipulation on ALLEX, a humanoid platform. I received my M.S. from Korea University and KIST, where I studied context-aware affordance learning for robotic manipulation.

My goal is to solve challenging Physical AI problems that help bring forward the era of one robot per person. Please feel free to contact me by email if you would like to discuss research topics, collaborations, or Ph.D. opportunities.
